Image Breed Description
Havanese A Havanese is a bichon-type dog and is the national dog of Cuba. This breed has many admirers because of their long, silky hair, and cuddly size. Havanese is a small-breed dog with a rounded face, floppy ears, and either curly or straight hair.
Pomeranian Pomeranians are admired because of their fluffy double coat and foxy face with alert, prick ears. Pomeranians are known for being smart, energetic feisty, and bold. Pomeranians are playful and loves to be the center of attention.
Chow Chow Chow Chows are powerful, compactly built dogs standing as high as 20 inches at the shoulder. Chow Chows are known as the bright, refined lion of the dog world. They are popular for their lion-like features.
